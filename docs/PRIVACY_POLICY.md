## 2. What personal data we collect

### 2.1 Account and identity

- **Email address** — When you sign up or use “Sign in with” (e.g. Google, Apple, Facebook).
- **User ID** — A unique identifier for your account (UUID).
- **Account type** — Personal or Business, as chosen at sign-up.
- **Subscription status** — Whether you use free or paid features (e.g. Super, Premium).

### 2.2 Profile information

Depending on your account type and which parts of the app you use:

- **Personal profiles:** Name, gender, date of birth, city, education, occupation, languages, bio, photos, interests, lifestyle and preference details (e.g. for Romance, Friends, or Business mode), and optional links such as Instagram.
- **Business profiles:** Business name, location, bio, tags, website, social links (e.g. Instagram, LinkedIn), and logo.

You choose what to add; we store what you provide to show your profile to other users and to power features (e.g. discovery, matching, AI suggestions).

### 2.3 Communications and content

- **Messages** — Text, images, and other content you send in chats (direct, group, event).
- **Planner data** — Titles, descriptions, dates, locations, and participants for your plans (dates, meetups, meetings, events).
- **Events** — Event details you create or join (title, description, location, participants, RSVPs).

### 2.4 Technical and usage data

- **Session and auth data** — Stored so you stay logged in (e.g. on your device and on our servers).
- **Analytics** — We use analytics (e.g. PostHog) to understand how the app is used. We do **not** send your name, email, or message content. We may send a user ID, account type, screen paths, and event names (e.g. “discover_open”, “recommendation_like”) to improve the product. You can use the app with analytics disabled if we offer that option, or we may rely on legitimate interest where the law allows.
- **AI usage** — When you use Winkly AI, we send only allowlisted information (e.g. mode, city, date range, activity hints, and a short summary of your and your partner’s profile for suggestions). We do **not** use your full chat history to train external AI models. We may store that you used a feature (e.g. user ID, mode, task type) for security and product improvement.

### 2.5 Device and permissions

- **Location** — If you grant permission, we use it for features such as city/discovery and event locations. Your location is **approximated before it is stored** (your precise GPS position is never saved), and you can choose **Approximate** or **Precise** precision in Privacy & Safety → Location precision. Other users only ever see a rounded distance, never your coordinates. You can revoke permission in your device settings.
- **Photos / camera** — Used only when you choose to add or change profile or event photos.
- **Contacts** — Only if you choose to use “find contacts on Winkly” and grant permission. Your contacts are **hashed on your device** (SHA-256 of email/phone) before anything is sent; we use only those hashed identifiers to find existing Winkly users and we do **not** store your contact list.

We do **not** sell your personal data.
