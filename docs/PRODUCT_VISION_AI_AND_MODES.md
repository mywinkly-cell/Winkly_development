# Winkly Product Vision — AI & Modes

## AI Concierge & Event Prioritization

### Core rule
**AI must prioritize Winkly events and business offers first.**  
- Suggest places, events, and activities that exist **in the Winkly platform** (business users, event organizers).  
- Only fall back to web search / external sources when nothing suitable is found in Winkly.  

### Why
- Makes Winkly an **advertising platform** for Business users (similar to Instagram/Facebook).  
- Drives value for businesses and events listed on Winkly.  
- Keeps planning context inside the app.

---

## Business Mode — LinkedIn + Instagram Mix

### Positioning
Business mode = professional discovery + direct contact + planning.

### Key features
1. **Search & filter**
   - Find people by role, company, industry, location, interests.  
   - Direct contact via 1:1 chat.

2. **Planning together**
   - Attend or create business events from Event mode.  
   - Plan a “business date” (e.g. coffee, golf, lunch) to get to know each other.  

3. **Example**
   - Two CEOs connect with a common interest (e.g. golf).  
   - Easily plan: find a golf event in their area or create one, join it, organize with/without AI support.  

4. **Events**
   - Winkly events (Event mode) are discoverable in Business mode.  
   - Business users can advertise offers and events specifically in Winkly.

## Mode-specific Chat Rules

| Mode    | 1:1 Chats | Group Chats | Who to invite                  |
|---------|-----------|-------------|--------------------------------|
| Romance | ✅ Only   | ❌ No       | Matches only                   |
| Friends | ✅ Yes    | ✅ Yes      | Matches + contacts (incl. invites) |
| Business| ✅ Yes    | ✅ Yes      | Connections + contacts         |
| Events  | ✅ Yes    | ✅ Yes      | Event participants             |

### Sub-profile requirement
- Before joining a group chat in Friends/Business, users must have the relevant sub-profile enabled.  
- If not, prompt: “Create your [Friends/Business] profile to join this chat.”  

---

## Friends Mode — Contacts & Invites

- Match with friends (mutual follow, similar to Romance mutual like).  
- 1:1 chats with matches or invited contacts.  
- Invite friends who are not on Winkly yet.  
- Connect contact list: Account Settings → Invite → “Connect contacts”.  
- Group chats: invite matches and people from the contact list.  
- AI concierge for group planning (weekend plans, meetups, events).
