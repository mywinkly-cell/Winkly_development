# Winkly — Community Guidelines

**Last updated:** 7 June 2026

These Community Guidelines explain how we expect members to behave on Winkly. They apply to all modes (Romance, Friends, Business, Events) and to profiles, chats, events, and planner activity. They supplement our [Terms of Service](/terms) and [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

---

## 1. Be respectful

- Treat others with kindness and respect, even when you disagree or are not interested in connecting further.
- Do not harass, bully, threaten, stalk, or intimidate anyone.
- Do not send spam, unsolicited commercial messages, or repeated unwanted contact after someone has declined or blocked you.

---

## 2. Be honest and authentic

- Use your real identity as required for your account type (Personal or Business).
- Do not impersonate another person, brand, or organisation.
- Do not misrepresent your age, location, relationship status, or professional credentials in a way that could mislead others.

---

## 3. Keep content appropriate

- Do not post content that is illegal, hateful, discriminatory, violent, sexually exploitative, or that incites harm.
- Do not share others’ private information without consent (doxxing).
- Profile photos and messages should be suitable for the context of the mode you are using.

---

## 4. Safety first

- Take normal precautions when meeting people in person; meet in public places when possible and tell someone you trust where you are going.
- Report suspicious, abusive, or unsafe behaviour using in-app reporting tools.
- Block users you do not wish to interact with.

---

## 5. Protect privacy

- Do not share another user’s exact home address, private contact details, or confidential business information without permission.
- Do not scrape, copy, or redistribute other users’ data outside Winkly.

---

## 6. Use the app as intended

- Do not automate access, scrape the Service, or attempt to bypass technical or access limits.
- Do not use Winkly for fraud, scams, or illegal activity.
- Business accounts should represent a legitimate business or professional presence.

---

## 7. Enforcement

We may remove content, restrict features, suspend, or permanently ban accounts that violate these Guidelines, our Terms of Service, or applicable law. Serious violations (e.g. threats, child safety concerns, illegal content) may be reported to authorities where required.

---

## 8. Contact

Questions or reports: **General settings → Support & legal → Contact Support** in the app, or email [customer-care@mywinkly.de](mailto:customer-care@mywinkly.de).

---

*These Guidelines may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Winkly after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Guidelines.*
